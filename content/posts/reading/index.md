Since late 2022, I've been tracking most of the books that I read. I've also
started posting a digital version of that list [here](@/reading/_index.md).
It'll receive periodic updates without mentioning, mostly.

Stumbling upon the
[r/BookshelvesDetective](https://www.reddit.com/r/BookshelvesDetective/) part of
Reddit a while back got me thinking about the monumental number of books out
there I'd like to read and how there always seems to be another one. My
[StoryGraph](https://thestorygraph.com/) to-read (more of a "might like to
read") list recently crossed the 800 book mark. I hope that you'll find other
books you'd like through it.
